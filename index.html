Powerful Features

Genuinely Free Forever

No catches herecompletely free to use whenever you need it. No sign-ups, no usage limits, no restrictive licensing, and definitely no surprise premium tiers. Just advanced theme creation tools available to everyone.

Accessibility & Dark Mode Harmony

Create inclusive themes that work for everyone with built-in WCAG compliance checks. Our intelligent shade mapping ensures color combinations meet accessibility standards across both light and dark modes, maintaining consistent visual hierarchy with automatic relationship suggestions.

Comprehensive Preview

You can even theme this application interactively. Just name your color palettes "primary", "secondary", "accent", and "background" to see your changes instantly in the Preview tab above. It's an easy way to realise you need to dial bright colors back a smidge.

Advanced Color Science

Our backroom boys know all about color science, so you don't have to. Create harmonious, accessible color palettes with ease like an expert. Generate balanced color shades, extract palettes from images, and visualize color relationships all in one tool.

Interactive Inspiration

Never start from scratch again. Browse our curated collection of color palettes from various design categories, apply them instantly to your theme, and tweak them to match your brand perfectly.

Made With Love

This app is free because we're passionate about creating tools that designers and developers actually love to use. We believe great design should be accessible to everyone, and your success is our ultimate reward.

Create Your Theme in 5 Simple Steps

New

Follow our streamlined process to create a professional Chakra UI theme with our redesigned interface.

Create Color Palettes

Build your brand's color foundation with our streamlined palette tools

  • Select a base color and let the advanced color algorithm generate balanced shades automatically
  • Create distinct palettes from images, our color inspiration tool, or thoughtful presets for all-in-one palettes for a ready-made theme
  • Name your palettes "primary", "secondary", "accent", or "background" to see your changes instantly in the Theme Preview tab!

Set Typography

Choose between curated font combinations or custom font selections

  • Use 'Curated Combinations' for pre-matched heading/body fonts
  • Switch to 'Custom Fonts' for full control over font selection
  • Preview your typography choices with real-time examples
  • Just like your color palettes, these fonts will be used in the Theme Preview tab

(Don't configure Spacing and Borders & Shadows just yet)

Wait until you're sure about colors and typography

Preview & Download

Visualize your theme with real components in both light & dark modes

  • Theme preview uses the color palettes and typography your assigned
  • Toggle between light and dark modes to check color contrast
  • Test your theme on different UI patterns for compatibility
  • Export your theme as working code when you're ready to use it in your Chakra UI project
